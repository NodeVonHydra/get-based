Why this exists

Because your doctor
has 7 minutes

You get your blood work back as a PDF. Some numbers are flagged. Your doctor glances at it, says “everything looks fine,” and moves on.

But nobody’s tracking your ferritin dropping for two years straight. Nobody’s connecting your fasting insulin to your sleep. Nobody asks about your light exposure, your water quality, your stress.

I built Lab Charts because health data shouldn’t live in a drawer. I wanted trends, not snapshots. AI that knows my full context — diet, sleep, exercise, environment, goals — not one that just compares numbers to ranges. And I wanted to own my data completely.

Privacy-first

Your data never
leaves your device

No account. No server. No tracking. Everything is stored in your browser's localStorage. Lab Charts is a PWA that works fully offline after first visit.

🔒

100% Client-Side

All data stays in localStorage. No server, no database, no cloud sync. Your health data is yours alone.

👁

PII Obfuscation

Names, addresses, and IDs are replaced with fake data before any PDF text reaches AI. Local AI handles all languages.

💻

Run AI Locally

Use Ollama to run the entire AI pipeline on your machine. Zero data leaves your network. Completely free.

🔒

Encrypted & Backed Up

AES-256-GCM encryption at rest with your passphrase. Automatic IndexedDB snapshots with one-click restore.
